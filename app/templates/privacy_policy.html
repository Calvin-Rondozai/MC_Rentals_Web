1. Introduction & scope

{{ site_name }} ("we", "us", or "our") operates this website to advertise houses and rooms available to rent in Zimbabwe, and to manage tenancies for the properties we own or administer. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you browse the site, enquire about a property, or become a tenant, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and the rights you have over it.

This policy is written to comply with Zimbabwe's Cyber and Data Protection Act [Chapter 12:07] ("the Act"), which is regulated by the Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ) as the country's data protection authority. It applies to everyone who visits this website or whose personal information we hold as a tenant or prospective tenant, regardless of where you access the site from.

{{ icon('building', 'icon') }} This policy does not cover the practices of third-party services we link to or communicate through, such as WhatsApp/Meta or your mobile network operator. Please read their own privacy policies before using them to contact us.

We collect different information depending on how you interact with us:

4. How we use your information

  • Responding to enquiries about property availability, pricing, and viewings.
  • Setting up and administering a tenancy once you move into one of our properties (rent tracking, lease dates, correspondence).
  • Keeping the website secure, diagnosing faults, and preventing abuse of our systems.
  • Complying with our legal, tax, and regulatory obligations under Zimbabwean law.
  • Improving the listings and content on this website.

We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects concerning you, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

5. Our legal basis for processing

Under the Cyber and Data Protection Act, we only process personal information where we have a lawful basis to do so:

  • Consent — for example, when you voluntarily message us on WhatsApp or call us.
  • Performance of a contract — to manage your tenancy once a lease is agreed.
  • Legitimate interest — to keep the site secure and functioning, in a way that does not override your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where Zimbabwean law requires us to keep or disclose certain records.

6. Cookies & local storage

This website does not use advertising or third-party analytics cookies. The only thing we store in your browser is a single local storage entry (mc-theme) that remembers whether you prefer light or dark mode — it never leaves your device and is not shared with us or anyone else. If that ever changes (for example, if we add analytics in future), we will update this section first.

7. Who we share information with

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it in these limited situations:

  • WhatsApp / Meta Platforms, Inc. — if you contact us via WhatsApp, that conversation is carried and stored on WhatsApp's infrastructure under WhatsApp's own privacy policy, which may involve processing outside Zimbabwe.
  • Your mobile network operator — if you call our published phone number.
  • Service providers — such as our website hosting provider, who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations, solely to keep the site running.
  • Regulators & law enforcement — where required by Zimbabwean law, court order, or a lawful request from POTRAZ or another authority.
  • A buyer or successor — if the properties or business are ever sold or transferred, subject to the same protections described here.

8. Retention & security

How long we keep it

Enquiry conversations conducted over WhatsApp or phone are retained according to those platforms' own retention practices, which we do not control. Tenancy and occupant records are kept for the duration of the tenancy and for a further period afterwards where reasonably necessary to comply with legal, tax, or dispute-resolution obligations, after which they are securely deleted or anonymised.

How we protect it

Access to tenant and occupant records is restricted to authorised administrators who must log in with a password that is never stored in plain text. Session cookies used by the admin panel are marked HttpOnly and SameSite=Lax to reduce the risk of theft. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk.

9. Your rights

Under the Cyber and Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about how your personal information is used (this policy).
  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information where we no longer have a lawful reason to keep it.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where consent is our basis for processing (this won't affect anything done before you withdrew it).
  • Lodge a complaint with us or with POTRAZ (see Section 11).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13. We may need to verify your identity before acting on your request, and we will respond within a reasonable time as required by the Act.

10. Children's privacy

This website and our tenancy services are intended for adults. Tenancy agreements require the tenant to be at least 18 years old (the age of majority in Zimbabwe). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
